    13тЖТ    13тЖТ
    14тЖТ    14тЖТ3. **`/api/admin/data` (GET)** тАФ Retrieves the latest 100 inquiries and 100 contacts from the database, ordered by creation date descending. Maps camelCase Prisma fields back to snake_case for the JSON response, and serializes Date fields to ISO strings.
    15тЖТ    15тЖТ
    16тЖТ    16тЖТ4. **`/api/catalogue` (GET)** тАФ Generates a PDF product catalogue using `pdf-lib`. Includes a cover page, table of contents, and per-category product pages with specifications. Imports product data from `@/lib/product-data`. Returns the PDF as a downloadable attachment.
